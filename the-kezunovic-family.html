The Kezunovic Family

Welcome!

This web site is dedicated to the Kezunovic family tree. Most of the family members currently live in Sokolac (Sokolac, Republika Srpska, BiH). The tree contains all members of the family since

Gavrilo Kezunovic

who was born in 1780.
